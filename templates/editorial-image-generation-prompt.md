# Editorial Image Generation Prompt

Use this prompt after drafting an article. Replace `test` with the complete article body, then generate three independent 16:9 images in three separate image-generation calls.

The generated files for this repository should be saved in the article directory as:

1. `thumbnail.svg` or `thumbnail.png` - the article thumbnail and first image
2. `diagram.svg` or `diagram.png` - the body diagram / illustration
3. `summary.svg` or `summary.png` - the closing summary image

In the article HTML:

- Set `<meta property="og:image" content="thumbnail.svg" />` when the thumbnail file is local to the article directory.
- Render the thumbnail near the top of the article.
- Render the second and third images in the article body with `<figure>` and `<figcaption>`.
- Do not combine the three images into one grid, collage, carousel preview, or thumbnail sheet.

- 構図のバリエーション: 全枚で同じ構図の繰り返しは避け、内容に応じて主役要素（メインビジュアル／巨大な数字／印象的な短文／箇条書き／チャート 等）を切り替える。世界観の中で『カメラの位置』『主役』を変えながら、読み進めるリズムを作る
